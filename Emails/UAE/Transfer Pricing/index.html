What is Transfer Pricing?
 
 

The objective of transfer pricing (TP) is to: Establish, document, and defend prices for controlled intra-group transactions which may include:

Ideally, the transfer price should not differ from the prevailing market price which would be reflected in a transaction between independent persons i.e. the transactions should be at arm’s length.

Business Features of Transfer Pricing
  1. Record keeping in respect of all controlled intra-group transactions.
  2. Filing of ‘local’ file and ‘master’ file for both entities is necessary.
  3. Record of company activities if operating in multiple countries.
  4. All cashflow-related documents concerning the business.
