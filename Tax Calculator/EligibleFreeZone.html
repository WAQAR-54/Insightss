Conditions to be eligible for Qualifying Free Zone Person:

  • Be a juridical entity incorporated under a free zone authority.
  • Maintain adequate substance.
  • Derive qualifying income.
  • Not has elected to be subject to the standard tax rules and rates.
  • Comply with the arm's length principle.
  • Maintain transfer pricing documentation.
  • Maintain audited financial statements.
  • Meet De-Minimis requirements for its non-qualifying revenue.
